# Motion

**From motion to resolution.**

Motion is a modern committee management platform for Model United Nations conferences.

Built by delegates. Designed for chairs.

## Why Motion?

Most MUN committees rely on a combination of spreadsheets, timers, documents, and manual vote counting.

Motion aims to reduce administrative friction by bringing common chairing tools into a single workflow while preserving traditional MUN procedure.

## Features (Planned)

### Attendance & Quorum
- Delegate attendance tracking
- Automatic quorum calculation
- Session management

### Debate Management
- General Speakers List (GSL)
- Motion tracking
- Moderated caucus management
- Integrated committee timer

### Voting
- Simple majority calculations
- Two-thirds majority calculations
- Resolution voting support
- Abstention handling

### Documents
- Resolution display
- Draft resolution management
- Amendment support

## Design Philosophy

Motion follows a simple progression:

```text
Motion → Formation → Debate → Resolution
```

The software should support committee procedure, not replace it.
